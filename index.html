About Stranger Skills Map

Ever wondered what your neighbors are really good at? Stranger Skills Map is a hyperlocal platform that reveals the hidden talents around you. From someone teaching chess in the park to a stranger fixing bikes on the corner, discover unexpected expertise within walking distance.

Learn directly from real people in real-time. No courses. No barriers. Just genuine human-to-human skill sharing with optional tips to support their time.
